‚ùì Help & FAQ
Answers to everything you need
How do I add my OpenAI API key?‚ñº
Go to AI Assistant or Settings and paste your key starting with sk- into the API key field. Click Save. Your key stays in your browser only ‚Äî it's never sent to Nexora servers. To get a key, sign up at platform.openai.com.
What can the AI Assistant do?‚ñº
The AI (powered by OpenAI GPT) can explain any coding concept, review your project ideas, generate code, help debug errors, give career advice, create learning roadmaps, and answer literally any tech question. It's like having a senior dev mentor available 24/7.
Is Nexora free?‚ñº
Yes, the core platform is completely free. AI features use your own OpenAI key, so you only pay OpenAI's standard rates (GPT-4o mini is very cheap ‚Äî usually fractions of a cent per message).
I'm 15 ‚Äî is this for me?‚ñº
Absolutely! Starting at 15 is an incredible advantage. By the time you're 18, you'll have years of tracked contributions, a portfolio, and real experience. This dashboard was actually built by Aniketh at 15. You're right on time.
How do Daily Challenges work?‚ñº
Each day you get 3 fresh challenges. Complete them to earn XP. Build streaks by completing at least one challenge per day. XP and streaks contribute to your leaderboard ranking and unlock achievements.
How do I connect GitHub?‚ñº
Go to Integrations in the sidebar and click Connect GitHub. This is being built and will use OAuth to securely connect your account. All integrations only read public data ‚Äî we never post anything on your behalf.
